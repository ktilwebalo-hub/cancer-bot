# General Cancer Awareness

## What is Cancer?
Cancer is a disease in which abnormal cells divide uncontrollably and can invade nearby tissues. It can develop in almost any part of the body.

## Types of Cancer
- Carcinomas (most common): breast, lung, colon, prostate
- Sarcomas: bone, muscle, connective tissue
- Leukemias: blood cancers
- Lymphomas: lymphatic system
- Melanomas: skin cancer

## Risk Reduction
- Regular medical check-ups
- Healthy lifestyle choices
- Understanding personal risk factors
- Avoiding known carcinogens
- Following screening guidelines

## Advances in Treatment
- Precision medicine
- Immunotherapy breakthroughs
- Advanced imaging techniques
- Minimally invasive procedures
- Personalized treatment approaches
